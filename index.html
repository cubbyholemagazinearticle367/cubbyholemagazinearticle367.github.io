📂 Managing Your Data

iExplorer organizes your phone data into clear categories. You can view your camera roll, music library, messages, and app data in a folder-like structure. Click any category in the sidebar to open the associated files. You can drag and drop items from your phone directly to your computer desktop.

🎵 Transferring Music

Transferring songs requires a simple connection. Connect your iPhone via USB. Locate the "Media" section inside the iExplorer window. Select the songs you wish to move. Right-click the selection and choose "Export to Folder." The files will save to your specified location on your hard drive.

🛠️ Troubleshooting Connectivity

If the software does not recognize your device, check the following items:

  • Confirm the USB cable works correctly with other devices.
  • Ensure your phone displays the "Trust This Computer" prompt when you connect it.
  • Close any background processes that might interfere with device recognition.
  • Restart both your iPhone and your Windows computer.
